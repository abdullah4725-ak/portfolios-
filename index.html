Process

How I actually work.

The happy path is easy. I build for everything else.

01

Map Every Failure Point

Before writing a single node, I document what happens when data is missing, when the AI returns garbage, and when the approver is unavailable. Most builders skip this. It's why their systems fail silently.

02

Architect, Don't Automate

A Zap that sends an email when a form is filled is automation. What I build is an AI employee with a strict rulebook, a human escalation protocol, and a decision framework it runs on every single interaction.

03

Enforce Rules at Architecture Level

No prompt engineering. No "just tell the AI to be careful." Hard rules. The system is physically prevented from guessing when confidence drops below threshold. The AI knows exactly when to shut up.

04

Deliver with Kill Switch

Every system ships with fall-back logic, an audit trail, and a kill switch where the stakes demand one. The system runs. You don't have to watch it.
