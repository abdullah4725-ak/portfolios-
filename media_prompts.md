# AI Media Generation Prompts

To keep your portfolio background and assets looking absolute cutting-edge, you can use these exact prompts in tools like **Midjourney v6** or **RunwayML Gen-2**.

## 1. Network / Abstract Data Nodes Backgrounds
*These are perfect for replacing the hero video or the process background layout.*

**Midjourney (Dark Mode Texture):**
> `/imagine prompt: A cinematic close-up of a neural network data structure, glowing purple and magenta nodes connected by thin fiber-optic laser lines, set against a pitch black void, extremely minimalist, shallow depth of field, 8k resolution, photorealistic, tech corporate aesthetic --ar 16:9 --v 6.0`

**Midjourney (Light Mode Texture):**
> `/imagine prompt: A clean minimal abstract data visualization, smooth white and light grey background, translucent frosted glass nodes connected by thin glowing purple lines, soft studio lighting, airy, enterprise tech aesthetic, 8k resolution --ar 16:9 --v 6.0`

**RunwayML Gen-2 (For Video Hero):**
> `Prompt: Slow, smooth camera pan through a microscopic 3D glowing neural network, nodes gently pulsing with purple soft light, pitch black background, photorealistic, elegant tech corporate video, 4k`

## 2. Service Icons
*Generate premium 3D icons to replace the standard image boxes in the "Services" section.*

**Midjourney:**
> `/imagine prompt: A hyper-detailed 3D icon of [INSERT TOPIC: e.g. a futuristic brain / a glowing funnel / stacked documents], floating inside a subtle frosted glass cube, dark purple and magenta glow, dark minimal background, UI design asset, dribbble style, perfectly centered --v 6.0`

## 3. Abstract Headshot Retouching
*Give your headshot a subtle "tech" aura.*

**Midjourney (using an image prompt):**
> `/imagine prompt: [URL to your photo] Professional studio lighting portrait of a man, dark background with subtle purple light leak passing across the background, sharp focus, corporate tech founder aesthetic, --v 6.0`

> [!TIP]
> **Pro-Tip:** Once generated, put the images through a tool like *Topaz Gigapixel AI* or a web upscaler to make them perfectly crisp on 4k screens!
