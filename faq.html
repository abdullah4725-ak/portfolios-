Do you work with non-technical founders?+
Yes. You describe the problem in plain language: what's breaking, what's slow, what you keep hiring people to do. I handle everything technical. You never need to understand what a webhook is.
How does the free audit actually work?+
It's 30 minutes on a call. No slides, no pitch deck. I ask you to walk me through one workflow that's causing the most friction. I map where it breaks, where you're losing money, and what the fix looks like. You leave with a written breakdown. There is no obligation to hire me.
How long does a build typically take?+
A single system (e.g. a Voice AI booking agent or a RAG knowledge base) typically takes 1–2 weeks from scoping to delivery. A full multi-system build (Voice + WhatsApp + RAG integrated) takes 3–5 weeks. Timelines depend on data readiness and how quickly you can review and give feedback.
What do you need from me to get started?+
Three things: access to the tools or platforms involved (e.g. your CRM, calendar, WhatsApp Business account), a clear description of the workflow you want automated, and someone available to review and test during the build. I handle the rest.
What tools and platforms do you build on?+
Primarily n8n for orchestration, OpenAI and Claude for AI reasoning, Twilio and ElevenLabs for voice, WhatsApp Cloud API for messaging, Pinecone or Supabase for vector storage, and Cal.com for booking. The stack is chosen for the job. Not forced to fit a template.
What happens when the AI doesn't know the answer?+
Every system I build has a confidence scoring layer. When the AI's confidence drops below a defined threshold, it is architecturally prevented from guessing. It tells the user it will connect them with a human, and simultaneously fires a silent alert (usually Slack) to your team with the exact query and full context. The handoff is seamless. The prospect never feels the gap.
Will the system work 24/7 without someone watching it?+
Yes. That is the entire point. Every system ships with fallback logic for failure states, an audit trail of every action taken, and a kill switch for high-stakes operations. An automation that needs babysitting is not an automation. It is a liability.
Do you hand over the code and documentation?+
Yes. You receive the full n8n workflow export, an architecture document explaining every decision point, and a short walkthrough video. The system is yours. You are not locked into working with me to keep it running.
Why no hourly rate?+
Hourly billing incentivises slow work. You are paying for a working system, not for my time. Every engagement is scoped as a fixed deliverable. You know what you are getting and what you are paying before anything starts.
What is included in post-launch support?+
Single System builds include 2 weeks of post-launch support. Full Stack builds include 30 days. Support covers bugs, unexpected edge cases, and configuration issues. It does not cover new features. Those are scoped separately. Critical issues during support windows get same-day response.
Do you offer payment plans?+
Yes, for builds over $2,000. Standard split is 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. Bring it up on the audit call and we will sort it out before any paperwork is signed.
How do I know this will actually work for my business?+
Book the free audit. I will map your specific workflow, identify your specific failure points, and tell you honestly whether automation is the right fix. If it is not, I will tell you that too. I do not take on projects I cannot deliver.
What is the biggest reason AI automation projects fail?+
No fallback logic. Most builders only cover the happy path: what happens when everything works. They never document what happens when data is missing, when the AI returns garbage, or when the approver is on leave. Those gaps are where systems fail silently at 2am. Every system I build covers failure states first.
Can you show me examples of systems you've built?+
Yes. The Featured System section on the homepage shows the Omni-Channel Master Controller, a live Voice + WhatsApp system with Intent Switch, Calendar Defender, and Liability Shield. Architecture breakdowns and live demos are available on request during the audit call.
